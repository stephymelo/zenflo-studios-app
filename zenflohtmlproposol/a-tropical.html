How we flow

A simple path from
seed to sold-out.

Four phases. Roughly 8–12 weeks for a full launch. No agency theatre, no slide decks for the sake of slide decks.

01 / PlantWeek 1

Discovery & strategy

We dig into your audience, competitors, and what makes you actually different. Output: a strategy doc you'll keep referring back to.

02 / SproutWeek 2–4

Brand & visual system

Logo, type, color, photography direction, packaging. Two rounds of revisions. We build for scale — not just a hero image.

03 / GrowWeek 5–9

Site & store build

Shopify or custom marketing site. Real copy, real photography, real inventory hooked up. Tested across devices.

04 / BloomOngoing

Launch & grow

Launch campaign, SEO foundation, paid + social plan. We stay on as growth partner for as long as you want us around.
