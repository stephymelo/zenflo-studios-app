Zenflo Studios is a small creative + development studio led by South Florida designers with deep roots in Latin America. We build identity systems, Shopify experiences, and growth engines for beauty + lifestyle founders who are tired of generic agency work.

We've spent the last five years inside the beauty industry — salon brands, indie skincare, hair-care launches, color cosmetics. We know how to make a 14-day Black Friday window earn out a year of work. We know what a fragrance brand needs to look like on a phone screen. We know which Shopify apps to ignore.

Our work blends the warmth of Latin design — confident type, real photography, a little swagger — with the technical rigor of a development shop that ships. The output is brands that look as alive as their founders.

§ How we flow

Four phases, eight to twelve weeks.

No agency theatre, no slide decks for the sake of slide decks. Just the work, done together.

Phase 01Week 1

Plant — discovery

We dig into audience, competitors, voice, and where the wedge is. Output is a one-page strategy doc you'll actually reference.

Phase 02Wk 2–4

Sprout — brand

Logo, type, color, photography direction, packaging system. Two rounds of revisions. We build for scale, not just a hero shot.

Phase 03Wk 5–9

Grow — site

Custom Shopify or marketing site. Real copy, real photography, real inventory. Tested across every device + connection speed.

Phase 04Ongoing

Bloom — launch

Launch campaign, SEO foundation, paid + social. We stay on as growth partner — quarterly retainers, no lock-ins.
